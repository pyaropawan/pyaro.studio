Pyaro Studio is a music video production house based in Nepal. We work with the country’s most celebrated artists — Prakash Saput, Bhupu Pandey, Sunil Giri and more — to create visual stories as powerful as the music itself.

From intimate acoustic performances to sweeping cinematic productions, we bring the same precision and care to every project.
